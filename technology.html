The Tech Behind the Green

Monocrystalline Solar Efficiency

Modern portable solar technology has undergone a radical transformation. Traditional solar panels were rigid, heavy, and fragile. Through the development of ETFE (Ethylene Tetrafluoroethylene) coatings, we have created foldable panels that are not only durable but also possess a higher light-transmission rate than glass. This allows our users to harvest energy even in low-light conditions, effectively decentralizing the power grid one backpack at a time...

Vacuum Insulation & Thermodynamics

The science of the "Dewar Flask" has been perfected in our product line. By removing all air molecules between two walls of 18/8 food-grade stainless steel, we create a vacuum that prevents heat transfer by conduction and convection. The inner wall is also coated in a thin layer of copper to reflect radiant heat. This is high-level physics applied to a daily container, ensuring that a single purchase can replace thousands of single-use bottles over a decade...
