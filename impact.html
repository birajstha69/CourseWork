Measuring Our Impact

We believe that what gets measured gets done. Here's a look at the real-world impact of our collective efforts to create a more sustainable future.

Carbon Sequestration Metrics

Every time you choose a sustainable product, you're casting a vote for a healthier planet. Together, we've already displaced over 5 million single-use plastic items and supported the growth of over 50,000 acres of sustainable bamboo forests. That's a huge impact, and we're just getting started.

A Note from the Founders

EcoMart was born from a simple idea: that business can be a force for good. As founders, we're committed to building a company that not only offers beautiful, sustainable products, but also gives back to the planet. That's why we're proud to partner with organizations that are working to clean up our oceans, reforest our planet, and bring clean energy to communities in need.
