Measuring Our Impact

The numbers behind the mission

Carbon Sequestration Metrics

As of 2026, EcoMart has facilitated the displacement of over 5 million single-use plastic items. By promoting bamboo-based products, we have supported the growth of over 50,000 acres of sustainable bamboo forests, which sequester approximately 2 million tons of $CO_2$ annually. Impact isn't just a buzzword; it is a measurable metric of success.

A Note from the Founders

Biraj Shrestha, Divyamani Khawas, Sanam Kurumbang, and Mik Limbu started EcoMart with a simple question: "Can we build a company that leaves the world better than we found it?" Our impact is driven by this collective vision. We don't just sell products; we fund ocean cleanup projects and reforestation initiatives. For every solar charger sold, a portion of the proceeds goes toward providing clean light to off-grid communities in the Himalayas and beyond.
