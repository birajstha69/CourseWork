Green Logistics and E-Commerce

However, e-commerce brings the challenge of "The Last Mile" delivery. Transparency and Blockchain

The future of sustainable business is transparency. We are currently developing a pilot program using blockchain technology to verify our supply chain. Every customer will be able to see exactly which farm their bamboo came from, ensuring that no old-growth forests were cleared and that every worker was paid a living wage. This "Radical Traceability" is the only way to eliminate greenwashing in the global market.
