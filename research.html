Research Objective

The objective of this research is to benchmark the current product page agains industry leading e-commerce websites(Amazon,Etsy and Patagonia) .By analyzing these competitors, the goal is to identify standard feature, design trends, and technological best practices that are currently missing from the project in order to define a clear roadmap for improvement.

Key Findings

  • Visual Design: Ranges from Amazon's clean/minimalist utility to Etsy's vibrant/masonry layout and Patagonia's sophisticated lifestyle storytelling.
  • User Interface(UI): All three prioritize intuitive navigation, but distinct approaches are used (prominent search for Amazon vs. minimalist layout for Patagonia).
  • User Experience(UX): Amazon focuses on speed/efficiency (one-click); Etsy on community/engagement; Patagonia on product quality/sustainability information.
  • Technology: Utilization of AI recommendations, progressive web apps, lazy loading, and sustainable web design practices.
  • Navigation & Features: Analysis of mega-menus, filtering options, wishlist functionality, and specialized content (like repair guides or seller messaging).

Conclusion

Our product page has a solid foundation with semantic HTML and clear layout. However, it lacks modern e-commerce features like filtering,multiple images, and accessibility features. Reference sites implement responsive design, advanced filtering, customer reviews, and comprehensive accessibility support. Key improvements needed: mobile responsiveness, filtering/sorting, search functionality , multiple product images, and better accessibility.
